Night Crew

Content

Night Crew is a a co-op horror game where players play as the night crew of a haunted mall, working together to complete tasks while avoiding the mall's supernatural threats. The game emphasizes teamwork, communication, and strategy as players navigate through eerie environments, solve puzzles, and survive against the odds.


This game is currently in development; all I can really share about this project is what is available on the public store pages, I can share more about my involvement post release :)
